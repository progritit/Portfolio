About me

From business operations to web development

I am an aspiring full-stack web developer strengthening my core web development foundations through The Odin Project, with hands-on practice in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Git, and GitHub.

Before transitioning into web development, I built a multidisciplinary background across economics, data analysis, training administration, and operational support. This experience taught me how to translate complex business needs into structured logic, optimize workflows, and communicate clearly across teams.

I also believe in using AI with intention to amplify learning, support problem-solving, and improve modern production workflows. My goal is to bridge the gap between technical execution and business reality by combining web fundamentals, analytical thinking, and intelligent automation to build clean, purposeful digital solutions.
